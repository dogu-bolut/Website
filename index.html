about me

Enthusiastic and driven Management Information Systems graduate from Akdeniz University with a passion for both game programming and web development. With a solid foundation in game programming spanning four years and a transition to web development, I bring a diverse skill set and a keen eye for innovative solutions.

My academic journey has equipped me with the knowledge and adaptability required to thrive in dynamic tech environments. I am energized by the challenges and intricacies of software development and contributing my expertise to multifaceted projects. I firmly believe in the power of teamwork and enjoy taking an active role in project development, leveraging my skills in Management Information Systems to effectively contribute to the team's success.

My experiences have nurtured a love for problem-solving, creativity, and a constant drive to stay at the forefront of technological advancements. I am excited about the prospect of leveraging my skills and passion for technology to contribute meaningfully to innovative projects and create impactful solutions.

In conclusion, my academic background, coupled with my hands-on experience in game programming and web development, has cultivated a versatile skill set and a proactive approach to problem-solving.
